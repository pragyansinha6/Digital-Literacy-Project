Email Etiquette Do's and Don'ts for College Students

Do's ✅ : 
- Clear subject line 
- Proper greeting (Dear Professor [Name])
- Complete sentences, no slang
- Concise, proofread
- Professional closing + full name

Don'ts ❎ :
- Casual "Hey"
- Syllabus questions
- Emojis or exclamations
- Multiple topics
- No signature
